About English Subject

English is one of the most important and widely spoken languages in the world. It is used as a global communication tool between countries, schools, companies, and technology systems. Learning English helps students develop speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills that are essential for education and future careers. English also plays a big role in science, engineering, medicine, and the internet. Most global information is written in English, which makes it very important for knowledge and research. Studying English improves confidence, communication skills, and the ability to understand different cultures around the world. It is not just a subject in school, but a key skill for life, success, and global connection.

Reading is one of the most powerful skills in learning English. It helps students understand vocabulary, grammar, sentence structure, and communication patterns.
Books contain knowledge from history, science, culture, and real-life experiences. Reading regularly improves imagination, creativity, and critical thinking.
Technology has changed reading completely. Students now use e-books, websites, AI tools, and mobile apps to learn faster and easier than before.
However, reading printed books is still important because it improves focus, memory, and deep understanding without distractions.
Daily reading improves vocabulary, grammar, writing, speaking, and confidence.
In conclusion, reading is a lifelong skill that builds knowledge and communication ability.
Grammar is the structure of language. It helps us form correct sentences and communicate clearly.
Present: I go / She goes
Past: I went / She went
Future: I will go / She will go
Rules: - Subject + Verb - Capital letter start - Full stop end - Correct tense usage
